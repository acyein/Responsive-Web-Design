How It Works

Step 1

Fold and insert

The soft OrganiCup unfolds to form a light suction seal, keeping it securely in place.

Step 2

Wear up to 12 hours

When inserted correctly you shouldn’t feel the OrganiCup. Go swim, do sports and forget all about your period. Also at night.

Step 3

Empty, rinse, repeat

Simple! Once you get the hang of it, you’ll wonder how you ever lived without it.
